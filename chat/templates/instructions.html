Voight-Kampff Test

home instructions rooms
  1. Players are assigned a role: human or AI. The role will be displayed on the righthand side of the screen.
  2. Humans answer questions. The questions will be on the lefthand side of the screen.
  3. AI have their answers written by an AI. Quickly type anything into the chat and click send. It will take a few moments for the AI to compose your answer.
  4. After everyone has submitted their answers, take some time to discuss your answers.
  5. AI players try to pass as human.
  6. Humans use their meat-brains to root out the imposters.
  7. When you are ready, take a vote on each player. If half of the group agree they are an AI, that player is eliminated.
  8. If you want to do another round, everyone click on the Next Question button underneath the question. You will be taken to another page.
  9. If your party is finished, click the End Game button to reveal everyones true role.
